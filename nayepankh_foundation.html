About Us

Born During Crisis,
Built for Change

NayePankh Foundation was born during the COVID-19 pandemic to serve the most vulnerable. Since then, we have grown into one of India's largest student-led NGOs — registered under UP Government, 80G & 12A certified, and affiliated with Niti Aayog.

Featured in The Pioneer, Dainik Jagran, Hindustan and more — our work has touched over 2 lakh lives. Service to mankind is service to God.

"If we all do something, then together there is no problem that we cannot solve!"

— Prashant Shukla, Founder & President
